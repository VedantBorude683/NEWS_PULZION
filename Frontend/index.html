Frequently Asked Questions

Is NewsPulse free to use?

Yes, NewsPulse is completely free for all users. You can sign up, track headlines, save favorites, and use all analysis tools at no cost.

What data sources do you use?

We use multiple data sources, including NewsAPI for global headlines and GNews for localized city-level news, ensuring you get a comprehensive view of the media landscape.

How is my data used?

Your data, such as your email, name, and saved favorites, is stored securely. We only use your email to manage your account and send the (optional) daily digest. We never sell your data.

Can I use this on my mobile phone?

Absolutely! The entire application, from the homepage to the dashboard, is fully responsive and designed to work beautifully on all devices, including mobile phones and tablets.
